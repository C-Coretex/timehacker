How It Works

From setup to structured day in minutes

TimeHacker's planning model is simple by design — powerful in practice.

  1. 1

    Create your tasks

    Add fixed tasks with exact start and end times, or dynamic tasks with a min/max time budget and a priority level.

  2. 2

    Set your schedules

    Attach a recurring schedule to any task or category. Choose the pattern — daily, weekly on specific days, monthly, or yearly.

  3. 3

    Let the algorithm plan

    TimeHacker lays out fixed tasks first, then intelligently fills every free slot with your dynamic tasks by priority.

  4. 4

    Review & execute

    Open any calendar view, see your full day at a glance, and mark tasks complete as you go.
